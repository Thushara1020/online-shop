Who We Are

Founded in 2025 by Thushara Bhawantha, CyberWave Computers has grown from humble beginnings into Sri Lankan's No. 1 IT Partner. With an initial investment of just 19,300 Sri Lankan Rupees (USD 60), we’ve transformed into a trusted leader in the IT industry, offering cutting-edge solutions and a commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To be the world's most innovative brand that never dies, driven by technology, passion, and people. We’re not just a business; we’re a community of forward-thinkers, delivering unparalleled customer service and high-quality products.

What We Do

At CyberWave Computers, we specialize in:

  • Computers, Laptops & Accessories
  • Mobile Phones & Gadgets under our CyberWave MobX brand
  • Security Solutions with CyberWave SecuX
  • Cameras & Photography Equipment through CyberWave CaptureX
  • Certified Used Products with CyberWave RecompX
